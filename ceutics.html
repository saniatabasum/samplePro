PHARMACEUTICS

Suspention

Pharmaceutical suspention is a heterogeneous system in which finely divded solids particales are dispersed in a liquid medium. Unlike solutions, where solutes are completely dissolved, suspensions involve particles that are only partially soluble or insoluble in the liquid.

Solution

Pharmaceutical solutions refer to the process of improving solubility, chemical stability, and organoleptic properties of drugs to enhance their effectiveness and patience compliance..

Emulsion

Pharmaceutical emultions are liquid disperse system that are used in a variety of ways in the pharmaceutical industry. Drug delivery, Drug protection, Drug taste, Drug absorbtion, Drug bioavailability.
